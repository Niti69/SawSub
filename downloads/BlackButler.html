ABOUT

“Black Butler: Public School Edition” ek manga aur anime series hai, jise “Kuroshitsuji” ke roop mein bhi jaana jaata hai. Isme, ek shresht parivaar ke naujawan aur unka bhayanak karyakarta, Sebastian Michaelis, ek public school mein chhup jaate hain, jahan ve anek aitihasik anokhe hadse aur rahasyon ka samna karte hain. Sebastian ki sahayata se, protagonist Ciel Phantomhive, school ke andar chhipe rahasyon ko hal karne ke liye apne vicharon aur samajh ka upyog karta hai, jabki woh apne asli uddeshya, uske shresht parivaar ke pratishodh ke liye, dhoondhta hai.
